AI introduction

We have created an artificial intelligence (AI) agent to detect skin diseases from images. Skin diseases are detected in a two-step process. First, the AI agent detects 22 visual observations, which we call concepts. These concepts are then used to allow the AI agent to reason about the label it suggests to assign to the image.

The AI agent was created using 114 skin conditions, but for this study, we are only asking you to label images of seborrheic keratosis and malignant melanoma. As the AI was created using a broad range of skin conditions, the AI may detect unexpected concepts for seborrheic keratosis and malignant melanoma. It should also be understood the AI agent can make mistakes.

In this study, we are evaluating the AI agent's capabilities. Please do not see this as a new tool for your work as it is not designed to be used outside of research.
