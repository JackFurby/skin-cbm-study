Study

This is a study to evaluate a machine learning model architecture called a Concept Bottleneck Model, and explainable Artifical Intelegence techniques. The artificial agent has been trained to classify skin diseases as either malignant or benign.

As a participant, you will be shown a series of images and outputs from the artificial agent and asked to interact with the model to help you classify the images. You will be briefed on the task when you begin the study. The study should take you no more than 30 minutes to complete and on completion, you will receive a gift card. Participation in this study is entirely voluntary and you can withdraw from the study at any time.

This study is designed and run by Jack Furby, A PhD student in the School of Computer Science and Informatics at Cardiff University under the supervision of Prof Alun Preece.

This study should work with most browsers. It will require cookies and Javascript to be enabled. We will track your activity within the study and do not track any activity outside of it.
